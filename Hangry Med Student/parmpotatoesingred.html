Parmesan Roasted Potatoes

Ingredient Amount Notes
Red Potatoes (skin on) ~3lbs dice to bite-sized
Parmesan Cheese 1/3 Cup Use grated matchstick parmesan, as opposed to the powdery version
Olive Oil ~4tbsp
Steak Seasoning 1-2 tbsp per taste Mrs. Dash is great to help control the salt level
Salt As needed per taste
