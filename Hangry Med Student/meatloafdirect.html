Meatloaf

    Directions: In advance of making meatloaf, combine sauce ingredients into a bowl and chill for at least a few hours, and up to 3 days
  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Comine all meatloaf ingredients into a bowl, mix
  3. Place meatloaf into greased loaf pan (we use a meatloaf pan with holes in the bottom to allow the grease to drain)
  4. Poke finger-sized holes into meatloaf and pour sauce over it
  5. Bake for ~75 minutes, cool when finished
  6. Serve; goes great with green beans and parmesan roasted potatoes!
