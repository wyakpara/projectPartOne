Medical school is hard. Eating right and staying healthy is hard. Doing both is practically impossible!

But it is possible. When eating healthy home-cooked meals became prohibitively time-consuming for us to do on a daily basis, we started meal-prepping.

What is meal-prepping?

Meal prepping is exactly what it sounds like. You prepare meals ahead of time, usually on the weekends and in preparation for the whole week. We find that it works best to prepare two meals at a time, and all of these recipes assume that two people are going to be eating these meals.
