Parmesan Roasted Potatoes

Ingredient Amount Notes
Red Potatoes (skin on) ~3lbs dice to bite-sized
Parmesan Cheese 1/3 Cup Use grated matchstick parmesan, as opposed to the powdery version
Olive Oil ~4tbsp
Steak Seasoning 1-2 tbsp per taste Mrs. Dash is great to help control the salt level
Salt As needed per taste
    Directions:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 F, line roasting sheet with foil
  2. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix well
  3. Spread potatoe mixture onto baking sheet
  4. Place in oven for 30 minutes; remove and stir; replace in oven for another 30 minutes
  5. Remove from oven, allow to cool. Sample, add salt and other seasoning as needed
  6. Serve
