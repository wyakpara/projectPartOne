Scrambled Eggs:

  1. take x eggs
  2. add x/4 teaspoons of salt
  3. add x/2 teaspoons of pepper
  4. add x/8 cups of milk
  5. whisk together ingredients
  6. melt a small pat of butter onto a frying pan on medium heat
  7. add egg mixture
  8. cook, stirring regularly, until eggs are firm
  9. add cheese, if desired
  10. serve with desired condiments or on toast.
