Scrambled Eggs DOS

    Ingredients:
  • x eggs
  • x/4 tsp salt
  • x/2 tsp pepper
  • x/8 cups milk
  • butter
    Directions:
  1. whisk together eggs, salt, pepper, and milk. Set aside.
  2. melt a small pat of butter onto a frying pan on medium heat
  3. add egg mixture
  4. cook, stirring regularly, until eggs are firm
  5. add cheese, if desired
  6. serve with desired condiments or on toast.
